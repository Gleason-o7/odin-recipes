Brownies

Ingredients:

    5 tablespoons (71 grams) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups (249 grams) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs plus 1 egg yolk, cold
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup (75 grams) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (63 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup (128 grams) semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F.
  2. Line a 8 by 8-inch pan with foil or parchment paper and spray with nonstick cooking spray.
  3. In a microwave safe bowl, add the butter and sugar. Microwave for about 1 minute, or until the butter is melted.
  4. Whisk in the eggs, egg yolk, and vanilla. Stir in the oil and cocoa powder.
  5. With a rubber spatula, stir in the flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt until combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Place in the oven and bake for 30 minutes, or until the brownies are set. Do not overcook.
  8. Let cool completely before cutting and serving.
